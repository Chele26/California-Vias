✦ Meet Laura

I'm Laura,
Your Bilingual Real Estate Advisor for Southern California

Laura De Hernandez, known as Tu Casa Con Laura, is a licensed California Realtor (CA DRE #01820066) with nearly two decades of experience serving clients since 2007. Based in Long Beach, she serves Long Beach, South Gate, Huntington Park, Los Angeles, and Compton — assisting buyers, sellers, and investors throughout Los Angeles, Riverside, and San Bernardino Counties.

Based in California, I proudly serve a wide range of communities, helping individuals and families find not just a property — but the right place to build their future.

My approach is personal, detail-oriented, and focused on delivering a seamless and professional experience tailored to each client. From first-time buyers to complex transactions, I bring knowledge, negotiation expertise, and a deep understanding of the market to every opportunity.
